## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|------------------|
| T-21-01 | Tampering | claim_dedup_ledger_entry's concurrent-writer race | high | mitigate | `INSERT ... ON CONFLICT DO NOTHING ... RETURNING` is atomic at the Postgres row level — two concurrent transactions claiming the same (collection, text_sha256) can never both believe they are primary; the losing transaction's own uncommitted insert is discarded by the conflict, never partially applied. |
