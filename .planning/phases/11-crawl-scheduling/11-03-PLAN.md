<objective>
Implement the SCHED-02 change-detection gate as a thin `recrawl_source()` wrapper in `pipeline/crawl.py`: seed-probe the canonical URL, normalize with the exact silver-stage `remove_boilerplate`, SHA256, and decide skip-vs-crawl BEFORE any raw write. WORM-safe by construction — unchanged dynamic pages never reach `put_raw` or the LLM gateway. A max-staleness override forces an occasional full refresh.

Purpose: Keep the corpus fresh without thrashing the immutable raw zone or burning enrichment spend on nonce/timestamp noise.
Output: `recrawl_source()` + `_signature()` in `pipeline/crawl.py`.
</objective>
