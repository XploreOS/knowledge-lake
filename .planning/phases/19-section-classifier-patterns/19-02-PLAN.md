<objective>
Give `DomainLoader` optional `filters.yaml` support (CLEAN-06): a new `DomainFilters` Pydantic model in
`domains/models.py`, an optional (never required-or-raise) load in `domains/loader.py`, and a new
`domains/healthcare/filters.yaml` fixture carrying the healthcare pack's clinical-code allowlist
(`ICD-10`, `LOINC`, `RxNorm`, section-symbol references, dosage patterns).

Purpose: This is the domain-pack-contributed half of CLEAN-06's allowlist mechanism that Plan 19-04's
`classify_sections()` will consume so a chunk containing `ICD-10 E11.9` or `Metformin 500 mg PO BID` is
never classified as boilerplate regardless of its substance signals.

Output: `DomainFilters` model, `DomainLoader.filters` attribute, `domains/healthcare/filters.yaml`, and
regression tests proving existing domain packs without `filters.yaml` (aviation, climate, local/*)
continue to load unaffected.
</objective>
